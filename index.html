What is Symantycs?

Symantycs is an early stage AI startup that focuses on leveraging technology like Natural Language Processing and Topic Modelling to develop Intelligent Examination Suites for paper based examinations. 

What is an Intelligent Examination Suite?

Paper based examinations are still common in lot of universities/colleges. The evaluation method is manual, where the teachers are expected to manually evaluate and grade the papers. Symantycs with its breakthrough technology allows teachers to evaluate and grade hand-written paper based answer scripts.

LEXEI

Lexei is an intelligence enabled evaluation engine for paper-based examinations. Upload your scanned copies of answer scripts and leave everything else to Lexei. The answer scripts are evaluated and graded in an instant. At the heart of Lexei is intelligent character recognition, natural language processing and topic modelling.
